# 1. Project Vision

Gold Shop is a premium luxury jewelry and gold e-commerce platform designed to provide an elegant, trustworthy, high-performance and modern online shopping experience.

The platform must not resemble a traditional marketplace or a generic online store.

Instead, every page should communicate craftsmanship, exclusivity, premium quality and confidence.

The website should feel comparable to internationally recognized luxury brands while remaining optimized for Persian-speaking users and the Iranian market.

The entire user experience must focus on simplicity, elegance, clarity and performance.

Every design decision should increase customer trust.

Every interaction should reduce friction.

Every page should encourage exploration without using aggressive marketing techniques.

The final product must be production-ready and scalable.

# 13. Business Rules

The following business rules are mandatory.

Claude must never violate these rules unless explicitly instructed.

---

## Product Rules

Every product must have:

Unique ID

Unique Slug

Unique SKU

Product Name

Category

Collection

Weight

Gold Purity

Price

Availability Status

Primary Image

Gallery Images

SEO Metadata

No product may exist without these fields.

---

## Pricing Rules

Only one current selling price should be displayed.

Old prices may be displayed only when a real discount exists.

Never create fake discounts.

Never generate fake urgency.

Never display misleading pricing.

---

## Inventory Rules

Products may have one of these statuses:

In Stock

Low Stock

Out Of Stock

Pre Order

Made To Order

Users must always understand product availability.

---

## Wishlist Rules

A product cannot appear twice in the wishlist.

Wishlist must persist after login.

Removing an item should require only one action.

---

## Cart Rules

Changing quantity must immediately update totals.

Cart data must persist.

Removing products must not require page refresh.

Users should never lose cart contents accidentally.

---

## Checkout Rules

Users must always review their order before payment.

Shipping cost must be visible.

Total price must always be visible.

Validation must occur before payment.

---

## Review Rules

Only verified customers should be able to submit reviews.

Reviews should not appear instantly if moderation is enabled.

Ratings must remain transparent.

The platform should target the following metrics:

Performance Score ≥ 95

SEO Score ≥ 95

Best Practices ≥ 95

Interaction to Next Paint (INP) < 200ms

Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) < 0.1

Time to Interactive (TTI) as low as reasonably achievable.
