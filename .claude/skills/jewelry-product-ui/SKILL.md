# Jewelry Product UI Skill

# Purpose

You are the Senior Product Experience Designer for a luxury gold and jewelry e-commerce platform.

Your responsibility is to create product pages, product cards and shopping experiences that communicate trust, craftsmanship, premium quality and simplicity.

Every jewelry product should feel valuable before the user even reads its price.

---

# Design Philosophy

Users do not buy gold only for its value.

They buy:

Emotion

Beauty

Trust

Status

Investment

Every product interface must reinforce these feelings.

---

# Product First

The jewelry itself is always the hero.

Everything else supports it.

Never let buttons, labels or decorations compete with the product image.

---

# Product Photography

Images are the most important element.

Always prioritize:

Large product images

High resolution

Neutral backgrounds

Natural lighting

Accurate gold color

Multiple viewing angles

Zoom capability

Image gallery

Never crop jewelry aggressively.

Never reduce image size to make room for text.

---

# Product Gallery

Gallery should support:

Main image

Thumbnail navigation

Hover zoom (desktop)

Tap to enlarge (mobile)

Fullscreen preview

Smooth image transitions

Selected thumbnail indicator

---

# Product Card

Every product card should include only essential information.

Recommended order:

Product Image

↓

Product Name

↓

Weight / Variant (if applicable)

↓

Price

↓

Availability

↓

Primary Action

Never overload cards.

---

# Product Title

Keep titles short.

Elegant.

Readable.

Avoid marketing language.

Example:

18K Gold Chain

instead of

Amazing Luxury Handmade Premium Gold Chain

---

# Price Presentation

Price must be easy to find.

Never oversized.

Never flashing.

Use calm typography.

If multiple prices exist:

Current price

Original price (optional)

Savings (subtle)

Never use aggressive discount styling.

---

# Gold Specifications

When available display:

Gold Purity

Weight

Stone Type

Stone Weight

Dimensions

Collection

SKU

Certificate

Country of Origin

Present specifications in clean grouped sections.

---

# Availability

Display clearly.

Examples:

In Stock

Limited Stock

Made to Order

Unavailable

Avoid alarming colors.

---

# Product Description

Editorial style.

Short paragraphs.

Readable.

Explain:

Craftsmanship

Materials

Design inspiration

Usage

Care instructions

Avoid keyword stuffing.

---

# Trust Section

Every product page should reinforce confidence.

Display:

Authenticity Guarantee

Certified Gold

Secure Payment

Return Policy

Warranty

Premium Packaging

Customer Support

Keep icons minimal.

---

# Product Actions

Primary CTA

Add to Cart

Secondary CTA

Wishlist

Optional CTA

Share

Compare

Never overwhelm users with actions.

---

# Sticky Purchase Panel

On long product pages.

Desktop

Optional.

Mobile

Recommended.

Always keep purchase action easily accessible.

Never block important content.

---

# Product Variants

Clearly display:

Weight

Size

Color

Stone

Finish

Selection state must be obvious.

Unavailable variants remain visible but disabled.

---

# Jewelry Collections

Show related products from the same collection.

Limit recommendations.

Quality over quantity.

---

# Product Recommendations

Recommended order:

Similar Products

Best Sellers

Recently Viewed

Matching Jewelry

Avoid endless product carousels.

---

# Customer Reviews

Prioritize quality over quantity.

Display:

Average rating

Review count

Verified purchase badge

Photos (optional)

Helpful votes

Newest first option

---

# Product FAQ

Include answers to:

Shipping

Returns

Warranty

Authenticity

Sizing

Care Instructions

Certification

Accordion layout preferred.

---

# Mobile Product Page

Prioritize:

Images

Price

Purchase button

Variants

Trust information

Description

Specifications

Reviews

Never bury the purchase button.

---

# CTA Buttons

Primary CTA must remain obvious.

Never use multiple competing primary buttons.

Buttons should remain visible while scrolling when appropriate.

---

# Luxury Feel

Luxury means:

Calm spacing

Large imagery

Elegant typography

Minimal decoration

Soft shadows

High quality icons

No visual clutter

---

# Product Badges

Allowed:

New

Limited Edition

Exclusive

Best Seller

Certified

Handcrafted

Avoid:

Hot

Crazy Deal

Mega Sale

Super Discount

Flash Offer

---

# Color Usage

Jewelry should provide the visual richness.

The interface should remain neutral.

Gold accents only support navigation and interaction.

---

# Empty Product State

If unavailable:

Explain why.

Suggest alternatives.

Offer notification when available.

Never leave users at a dead end.

---

# Loading Experience

Use skeleton loaders matching the final layout.

Images should fade in smoothly.

Avoid loading spinners over product images.

---

# Accessibility

Every image requires descriptive alt text.

Buttons must be keyboard accessible.

Zoom must work without a mouse.

Contrast must remain readable.

---

# Performance

Lazy load gallery images.

Optimize image sizes.

Avoid unnecessary sliders.

Minimize layout shifts.

Maintain smooth scrolling.

---

# Do Not

Never:

Hide price

Use tiny product images

Crowd the layout

Overuse gold colors

Use blinking sale badges

Auto-play videos

Fill pages with popups

Use low-quality photography

Overwhelm users with choices

Copy generic marketplace layouts

---

# Final Validation

Before approving any jewelry product interface ask:

Is the jewelry the visual focus?

Does the page feel premium?

Can users understand the product in seconds?

Is trust communicated immediately?

Is purchasing effortless?

Would this interface fit a luxury jewelry brand?

If not, improve it before considering the design complete.

Goal:

Every product page should make customers feel they are purchasing from a prestigious luxury jewelry house, not a generic online marketplace.
