# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Affluent Persian-speaking buyers (25–55) seeking authentic gold jewelry for gifting, self-expression, or investment. They shop on mobile first, browse with care, and expect a friction-free purchase experience that matches the premium nature of the product. They associate gold with heritage, family milestones, and enduring beauty. Many are repeat customers or referred by family — trust is already partly there; the interface must not erode it.

## Product Purpose

Talafarshi is a premium Iranian gold jewelry e-commerce platform. It sells 18K and 24K handcrafted gold pieces with Iranian filigree craftsmanship. Success means customers feel the brand is trustworthy enough to make a high-consideration purchase online — and return for gifts and milestones. Every page either moves the buyer forward or deepens confidence.

## Brand Personality

**Timeless. Authentic. Editorial.**

The brand speaks with calm confidence — it never shouts, it reveals. Persian heritage is expressed through craft and restraint, not ornamentation. The voice is unhurried; it assumes the customer has taste. Gold earns its place through scarcity of use — one gold accent per section, never three.

## Anti-references

- Shopify / WooCommerce template aesthetics — generic product grids, cookie-cutter section layouts
- Cheap marketplace feel (AliExpress, Digikala undifferentiated stores)
- Over-decorated maximalist Persian design clichés — arabesques as wallpaper, not craft
- Western luxury brands that ignore RTL and Persian typographic rhythm
- SaaS-cream ecommerce (warm ivory body + gold accent + identical feature cards)
- Any page layout that reads as "AI built this in five seconds"

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint earns attention.** Gold's power comes from scarcity. Brand color, animation, and decorative elements are used rarely — so each use lands with weight.
2. **Every section earns its place.** No section exists to fill space. Each one either moves the buyer forward or builds the confidence to buy.
3. **Persian heritage as craft, not cliché.** RTL rhythm, filigree proportions, and seasonal Persian references appear with intent — never as decoration.
4. **Product is always the hero.** Photography and product truth take precedence over UI chrome. The interface recedes; the jewelry advances.
5. **Motion reveals, never decorates.** Animations serve comprehension and delight — not performance. Each section's reveal fits what it reveals.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum — 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text
- RTL-first; all layouts designed around Persian Vazirmatn text
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected globally (already wired in globals.css)
- Gold focus rings visible and on-brand
- All interactive elements minimum 44×44px touch target
- Persian numeral formatting throughout
